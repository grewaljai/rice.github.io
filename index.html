Welcome to Rice Simple Sequence Repeats Tool

Microsatellite or Simple sequence repeats (SSRs), are the codominant markers which are distributed across both prokaryotic and eukaryotic genomes. It is widely used for genetic studies, plays an important role in biological functions and molecular marker-assisted breeding in crops. Molecular markers play extensively role in marker-assisted selection (MAS), species identification, fingerprinting, diversity evaluation and gene mapping for crop improvement. SSR markers are highly polymorphic as it is depend on number of tandem repeats. There are high chances of dissimilarity in number of repeats between species or individuals, thus this leads to the chance of getting high polymorphism between two respective individuals.
